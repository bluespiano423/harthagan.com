## How We Can Restore the Land Where We Live

From home landscapes to farms and forests, practical ideas for rebuilding ecosystems and supporting biodiversity.

I’m Hart Hagan, an environmental reporter and educator exploring how individuals and communities can help restore the land. This site focuses on practical ways to rebuild soil, support wildlife, and strengthen our food system.

---

## Where Restoration Happens

The work of restoring nature happens in three places.

### Landscapes

The places where we live — our yards, gardens, parks, and neighborhoods. By planting native plants and restoring habitat, individuals can support birds, butterflies, bees, and other wildlife.

### Farms

Agriculture covers vast areas of land. Regenerative farming practices can rebuild soil, improve food quality, and restore ecosystems.

### Forests

Healthy forests support biodiversity, protect watersheds, and stabilize the climate.
