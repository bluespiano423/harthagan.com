# The Next Section of the Homepage

Right below the headline, introduce your **three-part framework**.

## Where Restoration Happens

The work of restoring nature happens in three places.

### Landscapes

The places where we live — our yards, gardens, parks, and neighborhoods. By planting native plants and restoring habitat, individuals can support birds, butterflies, bees, and other wildlife.

### Farms

Agriculture covers vast areas of land. Regenerative farming practices can rebuild soil, improve food quality, and restore ecosystems.

### Forests

Healthy forests support biodiversity, protect watersheds, and stabilize the climate. Understanding forest ecology is essential to protecting these ecosystems.
